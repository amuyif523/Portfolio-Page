# Positioning Strategy

## Positioning Statement
> "I design and build high-impact technical systems with product and creative sensibility."

## Primary Audience
**Founders & Engineering Leaders**
*People who value the intersection of engineering reliability and product vision.*

## Message Hierarchy
1. **Who I am**: A creative technologist bridging the gap between engineering and design.
2. **What I build**: Systems that are performant, scalable, and delightful to use.
3. **Proof**: Case studies of high-impact work (not just code snippets, but outcomes).
4. **Contact**: Clear call to action for collaboration.
